Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 21, 2026

Welcome to Coloring Kid: Magic Paint. We are committed to providing a safe, creative, and private environment for children. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our App.

1. Children’s Privacy (COPPA & GDPR Compliance)

Our App is designed specifically for children. We do not knowingly collect, use, or disclose any Personally Identifiable Information (PII) from children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). We comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as names, physical addresses, email addresses, or phone numbers.

To ensure the best experience, we may collect Non-Personal Information, including:

  • Device model and operating system version.
  • Anonymous app usage statistics (e.g., which coloring pages are popular).
  • Technical crash reports.

3. Analytics and Crash Reporting

We use Google Firebase (Analytics and Crashlytics) to improve our service. These tools are configured to ensure child safety:

  • No Ad Tracking: We have disabled the collection of Advertising Identifiers (IDFA/AAID).
  • Data Minimization: Information collected is strictly technical and anonymous.

4. Photos and Creative Content

Any artwork created within Coloring Kid: Magic Paint is saved locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or have access to these images on our servers.

5. Parental Control & External Links

We do not include links to social media or external websites that are accessible to children. Any essential external links (such as support or privacy policy) and potential in-app purchases are protected by a Parental Gate (a task only an adult can complete).

6. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell, trade, or rent any data to third parties. We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect the anonymous technical data we receive.

7. Third-Party Services

The App uses Firebase (by Google). You can review Google’s privacy commitment for Firebase here.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage parents to review this page periodically for any changes.
