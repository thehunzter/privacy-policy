Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 1, 2026

Welcome to Coloring Kid: Magic Paint. We are committed to providing a safe, creative, and 100% private environment for children. Our app is designed to be a safe haven where kids can express their creativity without any data tracking.

1. Children’s Privacy (COPPA & GDPR Compliance)

Our App is designed specifically for children. We strictly comply with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We do not collect any personal information.

2. No Data Collection

We believe in absolute privacy. Coloring Kid: Magic Paint:

  • DOES NOT collect any personal information (names, emails, locations, etc.).
  • DOES NOT collect any device identifiers (IDFA, AAID, or IDFV).
  • DOES NOT collect any usage statistics or behavioral data.
  • DOES NOT use any third-party analytics or crash reporting SDKs.

3. Offline Experience & No Advertising

To ensure maximum safety, the app is built to function without an internet connection for its core features. There are NO third-party advertisements in this app.

4. Photos and Creative Content

Any artwork created within Coloring Kid: Magic Paint is saved locally on your device. We do not upload, store, or have access to these images. They remain entirely under your control.

5. Parental Control & Safety

We protect children from unintended actions. Any essential external links (such as this Privacy Policy) are protected by a Parental Gate—a math challenge that only an adult can solve.

6. Data Sharing

Since we do not collect any data, we have no data to sell, trade, or share with third parties.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage parents to review this page periodically for any changes.
